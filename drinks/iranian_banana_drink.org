* Iranian Banana Drink 

Credits: Got the recipe from Niloofar Yazdani

*Ingredients*

| Ingredient      | Amount | Unit   |
|-----------------+--------+--------|
| Well rip banana |      3 | pieces |
| Milk            |    350 | ml     |

*Recipe*

Breakup bananas and put in blender, pour over milk, blend till smooth.
